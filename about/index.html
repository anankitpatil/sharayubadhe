ABOUT ME

CONTACT

Hello, I'm Sharayu—a Communication Design student, traveler, storyteller, and documenter of stories, and a million things to be.

I am a fourth-year student at the National Institute of Design, Andhra Pradesh. I explore various fields and collaborate across disciplines, blending diverse perspectives to create innovative and comprehensive work.

Storytelling through visuals is at the heart of my creative process. I capture stories with my camera, drawing inspiration from the world around me and the thoughts within. Authenticity, empathy, and sensitivity are essential in all my work, as I believe they enrich the narrative and resonate more deeply with the audience.

My interests lie in print and publication, photography, cinematography, and graphic design. These mediums offer me the opportunity to blend creativity with technique, allowing me to express ideas that are visually compelling and meaningful.

Trimbakeshwar

This August, I traveled to Trimbakeshwar, a hilly town near Nashik, with my family—my mother, father, and brother. It was a peaceful and rainy weekend, perfect for spending quality time together. Amidst the rain and the relaxed atmosphere, I decided to take out my camera and capture some moments whenever I had the chance. I didn’t plan anything elaborate, just simple shots of the surroundings and the little details that caught my eye throughout the day.

Project type - Personal Project
Role - Cinematography, Editing, Colour Grading
Process - The time I visited Trimbakeshwar near Nashik, Maharashtra. I aimed to capture the town's surroundings on a rainy day, focusing on the atmosphere and details. I then edited and color-graded the shots to match the mood of the scene.

शांतता (Shantata)

While traveling back home, I visited a temple that holds a special place in my heart, as my Nani and I often go there together and cherish our time. I captured some clips and worked on the sound design for the video, aiming to showcase the essence of the place. I focused on the people, their movements and behaviors, the flora and fauna, and the beautiful weather that day. My goal in editing was to create an immersive experience, making viewers feel as though they are right there with us.

Project type - Personal Project
Role - Cinematography, Editing
Process - Observing the world around me has always brought me joy and tranquility. Capturing the simplicity of everyday moments and noticing the little details has become my way of storytelling through my camera. I focus on angles, lighting, and the essence of the place, always aiming to create images that people can connect with and relate to

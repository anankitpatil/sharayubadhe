The Pigeon Train

KHM is an exchange student program project where we collaborated on a soundscape designed by CAFA, China. Our project centered around a tale of friendship set in an unfamiliar city, where the characters faced persecution from authorities and found their own path. We combined paper cutouts, Blender, and 2D animation to develop the story, characters, and environments. Throughout the project, we continuously refined our vision, blending these elements to create a cohesive and engaging narrative

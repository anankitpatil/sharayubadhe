Luminous

Complementing the opaque, optical and layered properties of birchply with a clear medium in different patterns for different light interactions and effects elevating the material to new heights of sophistication and applications.
- NihalKrishna N B, Industrial Design, National Institute of Design, AP.

 

Project type - Collaborative Project
Role - Creative Director, Photographer
Process - This project began when Nihal, my fellow college mate from the Industrial Design discipline, asked for help documenting his product in an ambient setting. The goal was to showcase how the product would look and fit into its existing environment. The process involved a lot of trial and error as I experimented with different placements and settings, with no restrictions on creativity.
